## Why We Use AI

1. **Consistency**: Produce content regularly without relying on personal experiences
2. **Accuracy**: Focus resources on research and fact-checking rather than writing
3. **Objectivity**: Avoid first-person bias and unverifiable claims
4. **Transparency**: Be honest about how content is created instead of pretending to personal expertise we don't have

## Our Commitment to Quality

### Fact-Checking Process
1. AI draft created with reliable sources
2. Multiple independent fact-checks performed
3. Ghana-specific context verified
4. Claims tested against user experiences and public data
5. Final review for accuracy and tone

### What We DON'T Do
- ‚ùå Claim personal experiences we don't have
